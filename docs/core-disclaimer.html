How to Interpret “CORE”

CORE does not mean “safe”, “profitable”, or “recommended to buy”.

In Banana Radar, CORE is a historical state based on observed on-chain behavior, not a prediction of future performance.

Why a token becomes CORE

  • It was launched through a real liquidity pool
  • It passed baseline on-chain checks
  • It continued to exist over an extended period
  • Multiple on-chain snapshots showed no structural failure

CORE reflects persistence over time, nothing more.

What CORE does not guarantee

  • No guarantee of price increase
  • No guarantee against loss
  • No guarantee of liquidity permanence
  • No investment recommendation

Intended Use

CORE is intended for research, comparison, and long-term observation of token behavior. Users must make their own decisions and assessments.

Banana Radar does not provide financial advice.
